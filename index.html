About Us

Supima Cotton

We specialize in sourcing and exporting premium Supima cotton from India, known for its exceptional softness, strength, and color retention. Ideal for high-end garments and fashion brands globally.

Fabric Finishing

Our textiles undergo expert finishing processes such as biowash and softener treatments, ensuring smooth texture and enhanced durability tailored for international markets.

Design Integration

We collaborate with global clients to incorporate custom designs and prints into the fabrics, including digital, screen, and block printing, all crafted with Indian expertise.

Dyeing & Decoration
